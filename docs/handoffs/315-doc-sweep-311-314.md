## oddities.md

1. GitHub Actions scheduled-cron delivery is unreliable: ~20% of declared slots land on a good day, often hours late, occasionally zero in a day. Don't rely on it for freshness-critical pipelines. Vercel's declared crons fire reliably (8+ hit their slots daily, proven in HO 314).
2. Vercel's Data Cache persists across deployments. `unstable_cache` entries are NOT colded by a redeploy. To surface a backfilled row, you need an explicit `revalidateTag`, the TTL, or the next cron revalidate. Affordance: `POST /api/revalidate?tag=<tag>` (Bearer CRON_SECRET).
3. The HO 285 report catch-up absorbs `/api/sync` morning soft-timeouts across days. A missed report is retried on later sync runs until the gen lands, so a report can show up roughly a day late with no bug. June 8 was generated Jun 20 16:57 after the Jun 19/20 morning syncs soft-timed-out; it wasn't a cache or predicate fault.
